About

Why Bantu Tech Tour?

Bantu Tech Tour is a week long event focusing on bringing Entrepreneurs, Innovators, Developers from Namibia and Zambia in Southern African region to address issues affecting the region (Internet penetration, Open Web, Innovation & Open Source Cultures) and how the solutions brought up could leverage, revolutionize and establish active Mozilla communities and the region into an open tech hub.

Who is this tour for?

The event features various talks from Mozilla Rep, passionate Mozillians and (hands) on sessions in open tools offered by Mozilla (Webmakers Appmaker etc), Introducing Localization of Mozilla products, Firefox OS and lastly Web-makers.However this event is open to everyone who is passionate about technology, and making the Web an innovative platform and a place to find opportunities.
